Sequences now support 'no_gap'. You might want to review your sequences for this setting called 'Implementation'.
The company name is now a link to the name of the company's partner. You may want to review your company's partner's name.
The second line of the default report footer is now automatically composed. You may want to revert to what you had there in the old installation of OpenERP.
If you are used to hiding certain features, be aware that the old group 'Usability / No One' is now reused as 'Technical Features'.
